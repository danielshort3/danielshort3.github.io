Before you begin

Review how your data is handled

Job Application Copilot works only after you acknowledge these local-processing and optional-transfer boundaries.

  • Application page: Analyze reads the current page URL and form structure, including labels and nearby text. It also reads a visible job-description section when available. It does not read values already entered in fields. "Use selected page text" reads only text you explicitly select.
  • Local Ollama: Selected documents, verified profile and custom links, relevant custom thoughts (including compensation preferences), and field context are processed through Ollama at 127.0.0.1. Cloud and remote models are blocked, and Ollama cloud must be disabled.
  • Optional U.S. employment eligibility: Explicit work-authorization and sponsorship choices are encrypted locally and used only through deterministic Yes/No rules. Unlike other verified facts, they are never sent to Ollama and never imply citizenship or immigration status.
  • Encrypted storage: Sources, retained files, verified facts, custom links and thoughts, application packets, embeddings, citations, and drafts are encrypted in this browser profile. Your vault passphrase is never stored or transmitted.
  • Optional website handoff: Only after your explicit confirmation, selected job metadata and selected resume or cover-letter files can be sent over HTTPS to danielshort.me for final tracker review. Custom links, thoughts, and compensation preferences are never included.
  • Sensitive actions stay manual: CAPTCHA pauses filling. Consent, signature, demographic, identity, security-code, employer file-upload, navigation, and submit controls are excluded.
  • Employment-status boundaries: Citizenship, immigration or visa status, I-9/E-Verify, export-control, and protected demographic self-identification stay manual and out of model context.
  • Your control: The extension never submits an application and contains no advertising, analytics, or cloud AI integration.
