For better cutouts

  • Use well-lit photos with strong contrast between subject and background.
  • Prefer plain backgrounds; avoid backgrounds that match hair, fur, or clothing.
  • Keep the full subject in frame (don’t crop out hands, feet, or hair).
Why these tips matter

The AI creates a mask from visible edges and texture. Low contrast, motion blur, and busy backgrounds make it harder to preserve fine details like hair and fur.
